GigForte

You didn’t sign up for this. You didn’t practice all of those hours so that you could sit at your computer writing emails. You want to be at your instrument; arranging and creating. GigForte is here to help you handle the annoying side of bandleading so that you have more time for what’s important, the music.


Simplify the administrative aspects of bandleading:

  • Store and organize sheet music.

  • Keep track of venue and personnel contacts.

  • Store full dossiers on past and upcoming gigs.

  • Easily share clear, understandable gig details with personnel.



Get started:

  • Create an account.

  • Add some tunes.

  • Create a setlist.

  • Add venues.

  • Book a gig!
