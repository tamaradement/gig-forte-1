GigForte

An app for busy, professional musicians and bandleaders that want to spend less time at their desk and more time with their craft. You didn’t practice all of those hours so that you could sit at your computer writing emails. You want to be at your instrument; arranging and creating. GigForte is here to help you handle the annoying side of bandleading so that you can focus your attention on what’s important, the music.

Simplify the administrative aspects of bandleading.

Store and organize sheet music.

Keep track of venue and personnel contacts.

Store full dossiers on past and upcoming gigs.

Easily share clear, understandable gig details with personnel.

How it works...

1. Add tunes — Keep track of all the tunes you currently play, as well as tunes you've played for past jobs in a searchable tune library.

2. Create setlists (optional) — Once you have your tune library set up, you can make setlists for each gig you play, or reuse past setlists for similar or recurring events.

3. Add contacts — Populate your call list with other users. If your musicians aren't using the app, it's really easy to invite them with using the invite page.

4. Create and store gig dossiers — Store all of the critical details of your event in ONE place; location, start/end time, break schedule, setlist, personnel, and all the other extraneous details that need to be hashed out when organizing an event.
